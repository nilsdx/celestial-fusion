# 0 DEF Mag feeding guide

This guide aims to help people who just acquired a 0 DEF Mag and don't know how to feed it, with all commonly sought after Mag cell Mags guides.

## First steps

All you need is a @Bluefull ranger.

- Step 1: Feed the mag 19 antidotes
- Step 2: Put the mag in the bank and withdraw it afterward, then feed it 1 more antidote to evolve it into Kalki.
- Step 3: Feed your Kalki antidotes until it reaches level 35 and evolves into Mitra.
- Step 4: Feed your Mitra antiparalysis until it reaches level 50 and evolves into Kaitabha

Congratulations ! You now have a 0 defense Kaitabha with Mylla & Youlla and Pilla !
You can give it a mag cell right away and refer to the "What now ?" section to know what to feed your mag ! However, the following Mag cells will require you some more work. Let's go over them.

## Tellusis, Gael Giel, Recon

For these 3 you will need to get your Kaitabha to become a Kama, by having its POW above its DEX, while in a specific Section ID.

- Step 1: Switch your ranger's section ID to @Viridia (/sectionid 0), and change block
- Step 2: Feed your Kaitabha Dimates until it evolves into Kama. If your Kaitabha evolves into Bhirava in between, it's perfectly normal, and you can continue giving it Dimates.
- Step 3: (for Gael Giel and Recon only) Feed your Kama Dimates until it reaches Level 100.

Congratulations ! You can now give your Kama a Dragon Scale/D-Photon Core/6th Anniversary Cell to turn your Kama into these mags !

## Twin Sato

For this mag you'll need to get your Kaitabha to become a Sato, by having its POW equals to its DEX at level 100 or above, as a Female Force with the Section ID @Viridia, @Bluefull, @Redria or @Whitill.

- Step 1: On your Ranger, feed your Kaitabha Antidotes until its DEX reaches 50.
- Step 2: Feed your Kaitabha Dimates until its POW reaches 49.
- Step 3: Give it to a Female Force with the Section ID @Viridia, @Bluefull, @Redria or @Whitill and feed your Kaitabha a couple more Dimates to evolve it into a Sato.

Congratulations ! You can now give your Sato a New Friend and turn it into Twin Sato !

## Moro and Twin Wraith

For these two you'll need to get your Kaitabha to have its POW equals to its DEX to turn it into Kumara for Moro, and Naga for Twin Wraith.

- Step 1: Feed your Kaitabha Antidotes until its DEX reaches 45.
- Step 2: Feed your Kaitabha Dimates until its POW reaches 44.
- Step 3: Give it to a @Whitill Force if you're making Moro, or a @Viridia Force if you're making Twin Wraith, and feed your Kaitabha the last few Dimates to get its POW to 45, and evolve it into Kumara/Naga.

Congratulations ! You can now give Heart of Morolian/Pandora's Box to turn your Kumara/Naga into Moro/Twin Wraith !

## What now ?

For all mag cell mags, you can use the following items to raise the stats you need:
- POW: feed it Dimates
- DEX: feed it Antidotes
- MIND: feed it Difluids

When your Mag reaches level 200, you can raise its Synchro by feeding it Antiparalysis, which are the most cost efficient items to raise it. Be aware that Synchro will decrease in value as you play (notably by dying), so you will need to raise it more eventually.
