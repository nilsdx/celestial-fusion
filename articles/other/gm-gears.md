# GM Exclusive Gears

::headerbox{type="gm"}

Certain pieces of equipments are exclusive to [GMs](/guides/gm), and aren't present in the Destiny PSOBB database. These are gears not a lot of players get to see, so here's a list of what exists.

## Disclaimer

These informations may not be accurate as players only have seen glimpses of these, with only a few in the hands of regular players.

| Lion Hearts | [Yasminkov 9000M](/weapons/yasminkov-9000m) with a custom texture (with what seems to be a tribal pattern on it) |
