# Bat Wing

## Technique Boosts

| Technique | Boost |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| Jellen | Range +100% |
| Zalure | Range +100% |

## Variants

Bat Wing has 3 cosmetic variants that aren't available anymore outside of trading:
- Golden Bat Wing
- Cursed Wing
- Demon Wing

## Trivia
- Demon Wing and Cursed Wing are legacy Halloween drops that have the exact same stats and boosts as Bat Wings
- Golden Bat Wing were a previous rare drop that is an actual cosmetic to Bat Wing, before [Astral Wings](/barriers/astral-wings) were released.
