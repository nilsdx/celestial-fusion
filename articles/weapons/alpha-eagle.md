# Alpha Eagle

Alpha Eagle are rare mechguns that were only distributed to players who took part in the server's closed alpha testings.

They are upgraded versions to both [Desert Eagle](/weapons/desert-eagle) and [Mystic Spirit](/weapons/mystic-spirit).

## Availability

This weapon is currently unobtainable outside of trading.
