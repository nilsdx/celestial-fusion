# Dragon Sword

Dragon Sword is a rare Sword that looks like a katana.

Its special is the same as [Tsumiki-J-Sword](/weapons/tsumikiri-j-sword): when your PB gauge is full, you can use its special to deplete it and launch a powerful all-directional wave.

While [Tsumiki-J-Sword](/weapons/tsumikiri-j-sword) is buffed and thus is more powerful than Dragon Sword, the later find its niche in being a faster PB depleter to get your gauge lower but not 0 in order to trigger a 100 PB Mag Trigger faster.
