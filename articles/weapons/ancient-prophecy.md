# Ancient Prophecy

Ancient Prophecy is a rare Double Saber. Its special is unique as it casts a level 17 Zalure on multiple targets at once, making it a decent Zalure option for CASTs that play solo and don't have access to [Zalure Gun](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/ES_Gun) yet.
