## Availability

### Item Combination

Mille Marteaux can be made by a Ranger with at least 210 ATA by equipping either [Heaven Punisher](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Master_Raven) or [Ophelie Seize](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Last_Swan), and then using the left out weapon from the inventory. All stats from the equipped weapon will be transfered over.
