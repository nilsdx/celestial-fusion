# Arctic Faust

Arctic Faust is a rare Shot that is a direct upgrade to [Iron Faust](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Iron_Faust), but it is not combo locked anymore.

Its special is Unreduced Blizzard, making it a powerful crowd control tool for Human Rangers.
