# Gal Wind

Its special attack is a piercing wave that costs 7% of the character's max HP. This wave has heavy attack accuracy and power.

In combination with [Forbidden Grimoire](/units/forbidden-grimoire), it becomes a potent multi-target weapon for Forces, but requires more skill to be properly used as your HP are drained fast using it.

| Item | Effect |
| ----------- | ----------- |
