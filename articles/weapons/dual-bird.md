# Dual Bird

Dual Bird are rare Mechguns that are [Master Raven](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Master_Raven) and [Last Swan](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Last_Swan) combined together. It is not a Destiny exclusive weapon.

When [Jointparts](/units/jointparts) are equipped, they become strong long range single target weapons on Hunter characters.

## Set Effect

| Item | Effect |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| [Jointparts](/units/jointparts) | Range +30.0, Hits: 4-4-4, ATA +20 |

## Availability

### Item Combination

Dual Bird can be made by a level 100 Ranger with at least 210 ATA (or a [HUcaseal](/classes/hucaseal) with the same stats if equipping Last Swan) by equipping either [Master Raven](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Master_Raven) or [Last Swan](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Last_Swan), and then using the left out weapon from the inventory. All stats from the equipped weapon will be transfered over. The equipped weapon must be ground up to +9.
