# Hell Needle

Hell Needle is a rare Needle weapon. Similarly to Arrest Needle, it has a high tier special in the form of Hell.

When combined with Vector Scope, it becomes the best Hell weapon for Rangers due to its speed and its range being better than a Shot weapon.

| [Vector Scope](/units/vector-scope) | Range +105.0, ATA+20 |
