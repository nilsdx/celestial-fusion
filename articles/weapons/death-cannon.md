# Death Cannon

Death Cannon is a rare Handgun that looks like a hand cannon.

With Destiny PSOBB heavily favoring Berserk over Charge, it is outclassed by [Heaven Striker](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Heaven_Striker). Moreover, its projectile is also too low to be used as a trap shooting weapon.
