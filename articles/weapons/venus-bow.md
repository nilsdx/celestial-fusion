# Venus Bow

Venus Bow is a rare Needle that fires arrows that deals fixed amount of damages depending on the attack type, at the cost of the same amount of damages in Mesetas.
- Normal: 7 damages
- Heavy: 77 damages
- Special: 777 damages

It is a good finisher for enemies with very high DFP such as [Bubbles](/enemies/bubbles) or [D-Virus Launcher](/enemies/d-virus-launcher).
