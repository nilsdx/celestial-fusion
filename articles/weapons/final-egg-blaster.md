# Final Egg Blaster

Final Egg Blaster (also known as FEB) are rare Mechguns that are two Egg Blasters in one weapon.

They have the widest angle out of all mechguns, and their special, Final Explosion, triggers when the owner is bellow 12.5% HP, where every single shot will have another projectile added that explodes like a Bazooka on impact, hitting everything in a wide area around.

They're considered to be the strongest mechguns in the game, even without [Ignis Engine](/units/ignis-engine) equipped.

## Set Effect

Note: both Final Egg Blaster and Astral Dragon can benefit from set effects

| Item | Effect |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| [Ignis Engine](/units/ignis-engine) | ATP+100, Hits: 4-4-4, slower fire rate, longer range and narrower angle (no value) |

## Variants

Final Egg Blaster has a cosmetic variant that can be acquired by combining it with another item to make Astral Dragon.
