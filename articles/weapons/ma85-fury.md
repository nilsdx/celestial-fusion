# M&A85 Fury

M&A85 Fury is a rare shot that fires bright energy blasts to a single target.

Its specials at first does nothing more to the weapon, but when you equip it for a while, it'll start shooting large beams of lights that have infinite vertical range, and are more powerful than the regular attacks.
