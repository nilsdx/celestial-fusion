# Combustion Cannon

Combustion Cannon is a rare Shot that shoots a single projectile like [Burning Visit](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Burning_Visit).

Its special Overdrive launches a huge fire wave projectile that pierces through enemies and the environment, and has infinite vertical range, at the cost of 1/10th of your total HP.

While a strong weapon by itself, its main purpose is for Rangers to lower their HP at any time to empower other weapons.

## Known issues

- Overdrive's projectile can make a client lag on some occasions.
