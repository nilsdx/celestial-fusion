# Astral Claw

Astral Claw are rare Daggers. Aside from their Demon's special, they also offer powerful technique boosts as well as an HP boost.

## Technique Boost

| Technique | Boost |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| Barta | Power +100% |
| Jellen | Range +300% |
| Resta | Range +100% |
