# Taste of Affection

## Technique Boost

| Technique | Boost |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| Shifta | Range +100% |
| Deband | Range +100% |

## Combination

[Celestial Fusion](/weapons/celestial-fusion) can be made by any character by equipping either Taste of Affection or [Passion Haze](/weapons/passion-haze), and then using the left out weapon from the inventory. All stats from the equipped weapon will be transfered over. The equipped weapon must be ground up to +15.
