# Berdysh

Berdysh is a rare Partisan.

Its special is the same as [Tsumiki-J-Sword](/weapons/tsumikiri-j-sword): when your PB gauge is full, you can use its special to deplete it and launch a powerful all-directional wave.

It is the only way Rangers can deplete their special halfway, thus enabling invincibility juggling strategies for them. For Hunters, the faster [Dragon Sword](/weapons/dragon-sword) or far stronger [Tsumikiri J-Sword](/weapons/tsumikiri-j-sword) should be privileged if you have access to these.
