# V503

V503 is a rare unit that increases the chances of certain special attacks to hit.

It also combines the effect of [Smartlink](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Smartlink), removing the range penalty Hunters and Forces suffer from when using ranged weapons, including ranged specials (such as [Ultimate Double Cannon](/weapons/ultimate-double-cannon)'s Ultimate Destruction).
