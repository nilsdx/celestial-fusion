# Spectral Suit

Spectral Suit is a rare frame that makes the user semi-invisible when they're not attacking, similarly to [Stealth Suit](https://wiki.pioneer2.net/w/Stealth_Suit).

This invisibility effect brings two benefits, though these are only useful in Time Attack content:
- Removes the "slingshot" effect from ranged weapons: normally, Ranged weapons being fired too quickly after equipping them may spawn the projectile from the position where they were last equipped, effectively slowing down the time to kill a monster. This effect removes this issue entirely;
- As Warps's delay uses transparency, saves 5 frames when teleporting if moving, and 7 frames if standing still.
