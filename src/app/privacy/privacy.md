# Privacy policy

# 1. What Data We Collect

The only information we collect is the total number of views on any given page of this website.

# 2. What We Do not Collect

We do not track or store:

- Personally Identifiable Information : We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

- IP Addresses: We do not log your specific network location.

- Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies to follow your activity across the web or identify you on return visits.

- User Behavior: We do not track your mouse movements, click patterns, or time spent on the page.

# 3. How we use this data

The number of views is used to see which articles are the most popular, like in the [Main Page](/). This data is anonymous, and cannot be linked back to any individual user.
